About me

Hi, I'm Heiko Plucas, a customer-focused technical product manager with a passion for solving problems and creating value through agile methods. I created this website to share my professional experience and insights with others who are interested in technology and product management.

I have a strong background in product management, particularly in the area of business intelligence and data analytics. In my current role at Deutsche Telekom, I am responsible for driving product discovery for big data solutions that drive cost reduction, sales increase, and customer satisfaction.

I have extensive experience with cloud technologies, particularly Amazon Web Services (AWS), and I have expertise in data strategy, business intelligence and analytics, and data management using services such as QuickSight, Lambda, S3, Glue, and Athena. I am skilled in using data analytics on an Apache Hadoop cluster to create solutions that deliver real business value.

Throughout my career, I have held roles as a solution architect, product owner, and operations manager, and have worked with teams across the globe using agile frameworks such as SAFe, Scrum, and Kanban. In my free time, I enjoy staying up-to-date on the latest technologies through Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs) and other professional development opportunities.
